### **Problem Statement:**
Rural farmers across India lose over **Rs 11,333 crore annually** to phone scammers impersonating bank officials and government representatives, with AI-generated deepfake voices making these calls nearly impossible to detect. 

Over **68% of UPI fraud victims** are from rural areas, yet no existing tool works for low-literacy, Hindi-speaking farmers — by the time they realize it was a scam, the money is already gone. 

**VoiceGuard** solves this with a single button that listens to a live call, detects scam patterns in real time, and warns farmers in plain Hindi before any financial damage is done.
